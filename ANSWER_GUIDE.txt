Accessibility Escape Room 2026 Answer Guide

Question 1 | Room 1 | Type: defect
A photo that is important to understanding the page has no text alternative. What should be added?
Accepted keyword phrases: alt text, alternative text, text alternative, alt
Hint: Think about the short text a screen reader can announce for an image.
Explanation: Important images need a text alternative so people who cannot see the image still get the same essential meaning.
